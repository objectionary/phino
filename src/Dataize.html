-- The Morphing function M:<B,S> -> <P,S> maps objects to
-- primitives, possibly modifying the state of evaluation.
-- Terminology:
-- P(e) - is e Primitive, which is either formation without λ binding or termination ⊥
-- N(e) - normalize e
